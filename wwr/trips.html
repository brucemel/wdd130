Trip 1

Colorado River

Rafting on the Colorado River offers thrilling rapids, stunning landscapes, and an unforgettable experience in the wild nature. Participants receive safety instructions, proper equipment, and guidance from an expert before boarding the raft. During the descent, they paddle together to navigate obstacles while enjoying the rugged canyon and spotting wildlife. Breaks for rest and a lunch on the shore provide comfort and opportunities to immerse in the surroundings before concluding the adventure.

Trip 2

Boise River

Explore the Boise River for an exhilarating journey through nature's wonders. Equip yourself and navigate stunning landscapes and exciting rapids. Enjoy breaks for picnics and wildlife observation. Upon your return, leave the environment undisturbed. This adventure combines thrills, natural beauty, and serenity, crafting lasting memories. Whether seeking heart-pounding rapids or tranquil moments in nature, the Boise River delivers an unforgettable experience for all adventurers.

Trip 3

Lochsa River

A journey along the Lochsa River in Idaho guarantees an unforgettable adventure amidst pristine wilderness. Whitewater enthusiasts will be thrilled by its challenging rapids and stunning scenery. Prepare for exhilarating rapids, breathtaking forest views, and wildlife sightings. Whether you're an experienced rafter seeking excitement or a nature enthusiast craving scenic beauty, the Lochsa River offers an experience that will linger in your memory.
